# Contributing to Half of Nothing 🔥

Welcome! We're glad you're here.

This is a **vibecoding sweat shop** — we finish what we start, help each other, and ship real work together.

---

## Our Philosophy

> **What is half of nothing?**  
> We finish it together.

We value:
- Real progress over perfection
- Radical honesty with kindness
- Helping others as much as you get helped
- Shipping messy and improving later

---

## How to Contribute

### 1. Be Active in the Community
- Join the [Discord](https://discord.gg/GJQB5r7Wz)
- Post daily in `#daily-standup`
- Share your wins and struggles
- Help someone when you can

### 2. Contribute to This Repo
You can contribute in many ways:

- **Add resources** (tools, prompts, templates) to `/resources`
- **Write your member intro** in `/members`
- **Document a project** in `/projects`
- **Update the ship log** in `/ship-log`
- **Improve docs** (this file, README, etc.)
- **Report issues** or suggest improvements

### 3. Join a Group Build
Check the [Projects Board](https://github.com/329andalive/half-of-nothing/projects) and jump into any active project.

---

## Code of Conduct

We keep it simple:

- **Be kind and respectful** — No toxicity, no gatekeeping, no ego.
- **Give credit** where it’s due.
- **Be honest** about what you can commit to.
- **Celebrate effort** — Even small ships matter.
- **Help others** — Especially beginners.
- **No public shaming** — Take issues to DMs or moderators first.

**Breaking these rules repeatedly = removal from the community.**

---

## Questions?

Just ask in Discord `#help-me` or open an issue here.
