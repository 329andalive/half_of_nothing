## Welcome to the Vibecoding Sweat Shop 💪

A remote-first crew of coders, hackers, builders, and indie makers who are done starting projects alone and leaving them half-finished.

We leverage each other’s strengths, expose our weaknesses, and **actually ship** — personal projects, client work, open source, or crazy experiments.

**Motto:** *What is half of nothing?*

---

## Who This Is For 👥

- Solo devs in the boonies (or anywhere) feeling isolated 🏠
- People with half-built projects and full motivation problems ⚡
- Builders who want real accountability + honest feedback
- Beginners who want to level up fast by watching others
- Experienced devs who want to give back and stay sharp 🧠

---

## How We Operate ⚙️

### Core Rituals
- **Daily Standups** — Post what you’re working on every day 📅
- **Weekly Demo Day** — Show what you shipped (big or small) 🎉
- **Weakness Wednesday** — Ask for help on your gaps 🆘
- **Pair Programming** — Jump in voice and build live 👥
- **Ship Log** — Public record of everything we finish 📜

### Our Vibe
- Real work over fake productivity ⚒️
- Radical honesty + zero ego 💯
- Help others → get helped 🔄
- Ship messy, improve later 🚀
- No gatekeeping, no gurus 🚫
